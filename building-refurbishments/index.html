FAQ

Questions
we get asked.

Short answers to the things people most want to know before getting in touch. If yours isn't here, just call us.

Do you do garage and loft conversions?

Yes — both are core parts of our refurbishment work. Garage-to-room conversions and dormer/mansard/Velux loft conversions, all handled in-house.

How disruptive is a whole-house refurbishment?

We protect the rest of the house during the work, contain dust, and keep mess and disruption to a minimum. Most clients can stay in the property for partial refurbishments; for whole-house we'll talk through the phasing options with you.

Do you handle kitchens, bathrooms and staircases?

Yes — kitchen renovations and bathroom installations are some of our most-requested refurbishment work, alongside staircase replacements, gyms, cinema rooms and full period property restoration.
