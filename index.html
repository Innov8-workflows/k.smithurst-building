How we work

From first call
to handover.

A clear, four-step process that keeps every project on track and the client in the loop the whole way through.

01 / Talk

Initial chat

Call, message us, or fill in the form below. We'll book a site visit to see the property and answer any questions.

02 / Quote

Written quote

A clear, itemised quote covering scope, materials, labour and timeline — no hidden extras, no jargon.

03 / Build

Project managed build

We coordinate every trade and every inspection. You get regular progress updates and a single point of contact.

04 / Hand over

Snag & sign-off

Final walk-through together, any minor snags sorted, and full handover with workmanship backed up.
