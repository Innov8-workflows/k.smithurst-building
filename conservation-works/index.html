08 / Heritage

Built to blend seamlessly.

Heritage work is some of our most rewarding work. We've built full stone annexes that blend seamlessly into adjoining listed buildings, restored period property exteriors and worked through the planning conditions that conservation areas come with.

Limestone, sandstone, gritstone, lime mortar, traditional joinery, lead flashings, slate roofs — we know the materials and the techniques, and we're patient enough with the planning process to get the job done properly without taking shortcuts.

How it works

From quote
to handover.

A clear, ordered way of working that keeps the project on programme and the client in the loop.

01 / Visit

Site visit

We come and look at the property and any existing documentation — listed building consent, conservation officer notes, planning conditions.

02 / Plan

Quote & approvals

Itemised quote and a clear plan for the consent / building-control work needed before we can start on site.

03 / Build

Sympathetic build

Traditional materials and techniques where required. Sign-off from conservation officer where applicable.

04 / Finish

Snag & sign-off

Final walk-through, snags fixed, full handover with photos for your records.
