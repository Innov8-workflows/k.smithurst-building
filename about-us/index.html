How we work

From first call
to handover.

A clear four-step process that keeps the build on track and the client in the loop the whole way through.

01 / Talk

Initial chat

Call, WhatsApp or fill in the form. We'll book a site visit to see the property, understand what you want and answer any questions.

02 / Quote

Written quote

A clear, itemised quote covering scope, materials, labour and timeline — no hidden extras, no jargon, no obligation.

03 / Build

Project managed build

We coordinate every trade, every delivery and every building-control inspection. You get progress updates and one point of contact.

04 / Handover

Snag & sign-off

Final walk-through together, any minor snags sorted, and full handover. Workmanship is backed up after the project completes.
